Membership

Membership & Subscription Management

Full subscription lifecycle powered by Stripe

💳 Stripe-Powered Billing

Recurring subscriptions via Stripe Checkout with support for 5 plan types: Membership, Registered Trainer, Senior, Student, and Comped. Members subscribe, switch plans with prorated billing, cancel, and reactivate.

💳 Payment Method Management

Members add, update, or remove payment methods directly in the portal. Card removal stops auto-renewal without canceling — members receive reminder emails and an invoice they can pay anytime.

📄 Invoice History

Full invoice management synced from Stripe. Members view past invoices, download PDFs, and pay outstanding balances through a hosted checkout session.

🏷 Discount Request System

Students and seniors request reduced-rate plans. Admins receive one-click approve/deny email links that work without authentication — streamlined token-based approval flow.

🔔 Smart Renewal Reminders

Automated tiered reminders as expiration approaches. Failed payment notifications with retry guidance. Upcoming renewal alerts so members are never surprised.

📱 Digital Wallet Cards

Members add their NADA membership card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet with QR code for instant verification. Passes auto-update when subscription or certificate details change.

Trainings

Training & Education System

End-to-end training management for members and trainers

📅 Training Catalog

Browse upcoming trainings with filters for type (In Person, Virtual, Hybrid), price (Free/Paid), date range, and keyword search. Each training shows capacity, spots remaining, and a progress bar.

🎫 Registration & Payment

One-click registration with integrated Stripe Checkout for paid trainings. Members accept terms and the consent is captured with full forensic detail. Trainers are notified of every new registration.

📱 Training Wallet Passes

Save training tickets to Apple or Google Wallet. Passes include date, time, location, and trainer. In-person trainings get geofence alerts when you arrive. Virtual trainings include the meeting link on the pass.

👥 Group Training Requests

Organizations request institutional trainings via a public form. The system calculates fees using configurable flat-rate or percentage-based pricing. Confirmation and payment notifications are sent automatically.

⏰ Automated Reminders

Training reminder emails are sent automatically before upcoming events, keeping attendees informed and reducing no-shows.

📋 Clinical Submissions

Members submit clinical documentation with file uploads for trainer review. Trainers approve, reject, or request revisions. Approved clinicals can trigger certificate issuance directly.

Certification

Certificates & Verification

Automated issuance with public trust verification

🏆 Automated Certificate Issuance

Certificates are generated with unique 16-digit codes (XXXXXX-XXX-XXX-XXXX), branded landscape PDF via DomPDF, and stored for download. Expiration dates auto-sync with the member's subscription period.

🔎 Public Verification

Anyone can verify a NADA certificate at /verify/{code} without logging in. A trust mechanism for employers, institutions, and regulatory bodies.

🔄 Lifecycle Management

Certificates can be active, expired, or revoked. When subscriptions renew, certificate dates update automatically. When subscriptions lapse, certificates expire. Admin can reactivate or revoke at any time.

Trainer Portal

Trainer Ecosystem

A full suite of tools for NADA registered trainers

📈 Trainer Dashboard

Dedicated dashboard with training CRUD, attendee management, clinical review, earnings overview, and broadcast messaging — everything a trainer needs in one place.

👥 Attendee Management

View attendee lists per training, mark individual or bulk completions, export attendee data, and manage registrations across all trainings from a single view.

🌐 Public Directory & Profiles

Trainers appear in a public directory at /trainers with geocoded locations via OpenStreetMap. Visitors can contact trainers through a rate-limited form.

📣 Broadcast Messaging

Send targeted broadcast emails to training attendees. Live recipient count preview before sending so trainers know exactly who will receive the message.

💰 Stripe Connect Payouts

Trainers onboard to Stripe Connect Express for automatic payouts from paid training registrations. Configurable revenue splits with detailed earnings reports filterable by date range.

📝 Clinical Review

Review clinical documentation submitted by trainees, approve or reject with notes, and issue certificates directly after approval — all from the trainer portal.

Marketplace

Multi-Vendor NADA Shop

A full-featured marketplace with Stripe Connect payouts

🛒 Public Storefront

Browse products by category, vendor, keyword, or price. Each vendor has a dedicated storefront page. No login required to shop.

🏷 Member Pricing

Products support dual pricing: regular and member price. Active NADA members automatically receive discounted pricing at checkout.

🛒 Session-Based Cart

No account needed to shop. Cart persists in the session with real-time stock validation, quantity management, and separate shipping fee calculations for physical and digital products.

💳 Guest Checkout & Auto-Accounts

Guests checkout via Stripe. The system auto-creates an account with a set-password email, or links the order to an existing account if the email matches.

💲 Per-Vendor Splits & Payouts

Multi-vendor orders are automatically split. Each vendor's share is calculated and transferred to their Stripe Connect account immediately upon payment.

📦 Per-Vendor Shipping & Tracking

Each vendor manages their own fulfillment independently. Customers see per-vendor shipping status, tracking numbers, and delivery dates on their order detail page.

💾 Digital Products

Vendors sell downloadable files up to 50MB. Digital products skip shipping and are available for instant download from the order detail page after purchase.

💬 Vendor Contact System

Customers message vendors about specific orders through a rate-limited contact form. Messages arrive by email with the customer's address as reply-to for direct conversation.

🎯 AJAX Add to Cart

Products are added to cart without page refresh. The button shows a spinner, then a green checkmark confirmation, and the cart icon updates in real-time via Livewire events.

Vendor Portal

Vendor Management Tools

Everything vendors need to run their store

🛠 Vendor Dashboard

At-a-glance stats (total orders, revenue, active products, pending shipments), quick action links, and a recent orders table. Setup wizard guides new vendors through profile and Stripe onboarding.

🖼 Store Profile

Business name, description, contact info, default shipping fee, logo, and gallery images. Each vendor gets a public storefront at /shop/vendor/{slug}.

📦 Product Management

Full CRUD with multi-image upload (drag-to-reorder), dual pricing, stock tracking, SKU, category management, digital file support, and draft/active/archived status control.

🚚 Order Fulfillment

View orders containing your products, mark as shipped with optional tracking number (customer notified by email), then mark as delivered. Each vendor manages their portion independently.

📈 Earnings & Reports

Earnings overview with total revenue, platform fees, and net payouts. Detailed date-filterable reports showing per-order breakdown of subtotals, fees, and vendor payouts.

🔗 Stripe Connect Onboarding

Guided Stripe Express onboarding with status indicators (charges enabled, payouts enabled, details submitted) and a direct link to the Stripe dashboard for account management.

AI

AI-Powered Support Chatbot

Instant, contextual help powered by Claude

🤖 Claude-Powered Chat

Anthropic Claude (Sonnet) with a 594-line system prompt covering the entire portal. Multi-turn conversation with 20-message history. Rate-limited to 30 requests/hour per IP. Stays strictly on-topic.

👤 User Context Awareness

When logged in, the chatbot knows your name, role, subscription status, certificates, training registrations, clinicals, discount status, vendor profile, recent orders, and tracking numbers.

💡 Interactive Page Highlighting

Ask "Where do I find X?" and the chatbot highlights the actual element on the page with a smooth scroll and overlay animation. 31 highlightable elements across 12 pages plus full sidebar navigation.

Legal

Consent Tracking & Dispute Defense

Forensic-grade proof for every agreement acceptance

📝 Versioned Agreements

NDA and Terms of Service are versioned. Users must accept the current version after login. The middleware chain enforces: auth → email verified → NDA accepted before portal access.

🔎 Forensic Consent Capture

Every consent event records: user, agreement version, IP address, user agent, consent context (subscription, plan switch, training, trainer application), the related entity, dollar amount, Stripe transaction ID, and a full snapshot of the agreement text.

📄 PDF Evidence Generation

One-click branded PDF export from the admin panel with all consent details — ready to submit to Stripe for chargeback defense. Named by user and agreement for easy filing.
